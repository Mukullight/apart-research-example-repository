# Cost Management Plan: AI Safety Fellowship (Apart Research Model)

**Project:** AI Safety Fellowship – Pilot Cohort  
**Date:** 2026-06-11  
**Version:** 1.0  

---

## 1. Purpose
This plan describes how project costs will be estimated, budgeted, managed, monitored, and controlled to deliver the pilot fellowship within the **total** budget, while staying aligned with the lean Apart Research model.

## 2. Cost Estimation Methodology
- **Analogous & Bottom‑Up:**  
  - Stipend costs are estimated based on a tiered model  as used by Apart Research.  
  - Mentor honoraria match typical community rates.  
  - Other costs are bottom‑up estimates from vendor quotes or known free‑tier limits.
- **Reserve:** A 10% contingency  is included for unforeseen needs.
- **Currency:** All estimates in USD.

## 4. Funding & Cash Flow
-  seed donation confirmed in principle, released in two tranches:  
  - 60% at charter approval (01 Aug 2026)  
  - 40% at program start (15 Oct 2026)  
- Stipends are paid monthly; mentor honoraria at program end.
- Any unused contingency returns to the sponsor.

## 5. Cost Control
### 5.1 Tracking
- All expenses tracked against the baseline in a shared Airtable ledger.
- Program Lead approves expenses ≤ $200; Sponsor co‑approves > $200.

### 5.2 Variance Analysis
- Monthly cost reviews compare planned vs. actual.
  - **Cost Variance (CV)** = Earned Value – Actual Cost.  
  - **Cost Performance Index (CPI)** = EV / AC.  
- Thresholds: CPI < 0.9 or cumulative overrun > 5% triggers corrective action and sponsor notification.

### 5.3 Change Control
- Reallocations between categories > x or use of contingency reserve require a formal Change Request approved by the Sponsor.

## 6. Roles & Responsibilities
| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| Project Sponsor | Approves budget baseline, changes, and release of contingency. |
| Program Lead | Tracks daily costs, approves small expenses, reports monthly. |
| Finance/Ops (fiscal sponsor) | Processes payments, provides reconciled statements. |

## 7. Financial Reporting
- **Internal:** Monthly cost report (actual vs. budget, CPI, forecast).
- **Donor:** Quarterly narrative + financial summary; final pilot report includes full reconciliation.
