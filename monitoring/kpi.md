## Detailed Metrics Definitions

| KPI | Calculation / Data Source | Frequency | Responsible |
|-----|---------------------------|-----------|-------------|
| Fellow Research Output | (# of fellows who submitted paper to symposium) / (total fellows) | End of program | Program Lead |
| Fellow Retention Rate | (# of fellows active in Week 12) / (15 enrolled) | Weekly | Program Lead |
| Fellow NPS | Standard NPS survey: "How likely to recommend?" (0‑10 scale) | Mid‑program & end | Program Lead |
| Mentor NPS | Standard NPS survey to mentors | Mid‑program & end | Mentorship Coord. |
| On‑time Stipend Payments | (# of payments processed by 5th of month) / (total scheduled payments) | Monthly | Ops/Finance |
| Budget Variance (CPI) | Earned Value / Actual Cost | Monthly | Program Lead |
| Schedule Performance (SPI) | Earned Value / Planned Value | Monthly | Program Lead |
| Applications Received | Count from Airtable/Google Forms | Weekly during recruitment | Ops Coord. |
| Mentor Engagement | (# of scheduled 1‑on‑1s completed) / (total scheduled) | Weekly | Mentorship Coord. |

---

## Dashboard Notes & Thresholds
- Metrics are reviewed in the weekly team stand‑up.  
- Any KPI in **red** or **yellow** for two consecutive weeks triggers a root‑cause analysis and a corrective action proposal.  
- NPS surveys are conducted anonymously; verbatim comments are shared (with permission) to improve the program.  
- Fellow and mentor NPS will be gathered at Week 6 (mid‑program) and Week 12 (final).  
- Budget and schedule performance (CPI/SPI) are calculated monthly after financial reconciliation.
