## Risk Triggers & Escalation
- **Review Trigger:** Any risk score change ±3 points from last review must be discussed in the next CCB meeting.
- **Escalation Path:** Risks with a score ≥15 (High) are immediately escalated to the Sponsor and Advisory Board.
- **Monthly Deep Dive:** Sponsor reviews the top 3 risks and mitigation effectiveness.
