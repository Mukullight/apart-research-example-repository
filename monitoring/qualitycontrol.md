## Quality Standards & Metrics

| Deliverable / Process | Quality Standard | Acceptance Criteria | Inspection Method | Frequency | Responsible |
|------------------------|------------------|---------------------|-------------------|-----------|-------------|
| Research papers (fellows) | Clear argument, technical rigour, safety relevance | ≥ 2 mentors approve paper for symposium; passes plagiarism check | Mentor review + peer feedback | Once, before symposium | Mentorship Coord. |
| Mentor sessions | Regular, structured, supportive | 90% of scheduled sessions completed; fellow feedback ≥ 4/5 | Fellow survey, session log | Weekly | Mentorship Coord. |
| Stipend payments | Accurate amount, on‑time | 100% payments sent by 5th of month; correct tier applied | Finance reconciliation | Monthly | Ops/Finance |
| Application review | Fair, consistent, documented | All applications scored with rubric; ≥2 reviewers per applicant | Rubric audit (sample 10%) | Once, after selection | Selection Cmte. |
| Weekly seminars | High attendance, engaging content | ≥80% attendance; speaker/facilitator rating ≥ 4/5 | Attendance log, quick poll | Weekly | Program Lead |
| Final symposium | Smooth tech, on schedule, good attendance | ≥80% of fellows present; <2 tech disruptions | Run of show review, post‑event survey | Once | Program Lead |

## Quality Review Meetings
- **Frequency:** Weekly team stand‑up includes a 5‑minute quality check.  
- **Monthly:** Full quality review with Sponsor, focusing on key metrics (paper quality, NPS, payment accuracy).  
- **End‑of‑Program:** Retrospective and lessons learned on quality processes.

---

*Quality control is everyone’s responsibility. Report any quality concern immediately to the Program Lead.*
