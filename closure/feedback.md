# Participant Feedback Summary: AI Safety Fellowship (Pilot)

**Collection Dates:** 15–20 January 2027  
**Method:** Anonymous online survey (Google Forms)  
**Respondents:** 14 Fellows (100% of completers), 7 Mentors (87.5%)  

---

## 1. Fellow Feedback

### Quantitative Results (Average on 1–5 scale, unless noted)
| Question | Avg. Score |
|----------|------------|
| Overall satisfaction with the program | 4.6 |
| Relevance of curriculum to your AI safety interests | 4.4 |
| Quality of mentor matching | 4.7 |
| Usefulness of weekly seminars | 4.2 |
| Supportiveness of the community (Slack/co‑working) | 4.5 |
| Stipend adequacy (relative to time commitment) | 3.9 |
| Likelihood to recommend the program (NPS) | 72 (Promoters: 11, Passives: 2, Detractors: 1) |

### Qualitative Themes
**What worked well:**
- “My mentor was incredible – gave detailed paper feedback and connected me with collaborators.”  
- “Part‑time remote format allowed me to keep my job while doing meaningful safety research.”  
- “Final symposium felt like a real academic conference; I got great questions and ideas.”

**What to improve:**
- “More structured peer feedback sessions would have helped earlier drafts.”  
- “A little more guidance on choosing a research topic in the first 2 weeks.”  
- “One fellow suggested a dedicated co‑working hour slot for timezone clusters.”

---

## 2. Mentor Feedback

### Quantitative Results
| Question | Avg. Score |
|----------|------------|
| Overall satisfaction with the mentoring experience | 4.3 |
| Clarity of mentor expectations and guidelines | 4.0 |
| Fellow preparedness and responsiveness | 4.1 |
| Program support (mentor coordinator, resources) | 4.5 |
| Likelihood to mentor again (NPS) | 58 (Promoters: 5, Passives: 2, Detractors: 0) |

### Qualitative Themes
**What worked well:**
- “Honorarium was a nice recognition; more importantly, I enjoyed seeing my mentee grow.”  
- “Mentor coordinator promptly resolved scheduling conflicts.”

**What to improve:**
- “Could provide a mentor handbook with example feedback patterns.”  
- “A mid‑point mentor meetup to share tips would be valuable.”
