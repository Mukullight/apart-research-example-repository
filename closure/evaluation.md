# Project Evaluation Report: AI Safety Fellowship (Pilot)

**Project:** AI Safety Fellowship – Pilot Cohort  
**Date:** 30 January 2027  
**Prepared by:** Program Lead  
**Review Period:** 15 Oct 2026 – 15 Jan 2027  

---

## 1. Executive Summary
The pilot exceeded its primary objectives. 14 of 15 fellows completed the program (93% retention), all produced research papers, and both fellow and mentor satisfaction were high (NPS 72 and 58 respectively). The program stayed on schedule and 3% under budget. The model is validated for scaling to two cohorts per year.

## 2. Performance Against Success Criteria
| Success Criterion | Target | Actual | Met? |
|-------------------|--------|--------|------|
| Fellow retention rate | > 85% | 93% (14/15) | ✅ |
| Research output (% fellows submitting paper) | ≥ 80% | 100% (14/14*) | ✅ |
| Fellow NPS | ≥ 50 | 72 | ✅ |
| Mentor NPS | ≥ 50 | 58 | ✅ |
| On‑time stipend payments | 100% | 100% | ✅ |
| Budget (within baseline) | ≤ $43,450 | $42,180 | ✅ |
| Schedule (completion by 30 Jan) | On time | 30 Jan 2027 | ✅ |

\* *One fellow withdrew in Week 2 for personal reasons.*

## 3. Outcome Analysis
- **Research Quality:** 5 papers accepted at workshops (NeurIPS Safety Workshop, AAAI Ethics) within 2 months. Quality rated “good” or “excellent” by 85% of mentors.
- **Community:** Alumni Slack remains highly active; 8 fellows have volunteered as peer mentors for Cohort 2.
- **Equity & Inclusion:** Tiered stipends removed financial barriers; fellows from 12 countries, 47% women/non‑binary.
- **Cost Efficiency:** At $2,812 per fellow (total program cost / 15), the model is 40% cheaper than comparable residential programs.

## 4. Lessons Learned
| Area | Finding | Recommendation for Next Cohort |
|------|---------|-------------------------------|
| Recruitment | 120 applications received; quality high. | Extend promotion to non‑English speaking communities. |
| Mentorship | 1‑on‑1 highly valued; a few scheduling conflicts. | Formalise backup mentor pairing from week 1. |
| Curriculum | 12 weeks felt slightly rushed for 3 fellows with full‑time jobs. | Offer optional 14‑week extended track. |
| Tech & Tools | Notion overload for some fellows. | Simplify to fewer central tools. |

## 5. Stakeholder Feedback Summary
(Detailed feedback in `feedback.md`)
- **Fellows:** Praised mentorship, flexible format; requested more peer collaboration.
- **Mentors:** Found experience rewarding; suggested clearer guidelines on feedback depth.
- **Donor:** Impressed with cost efficiency and output; committed to supporting Cohort 2.

## 6. Continuation Recommendation
Based on the strong results, it is recommended to:
- Launch **Cohort 2** in April 2027 with the same core model.
- Scale to **20 fellows** with 10 mentors.
- Secure multi‑year funding based on pilot evidence.
- Establish an independent alumni‑led research community.
