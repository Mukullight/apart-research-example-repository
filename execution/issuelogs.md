## Escalation Path
- **Low/Medium priority:** Resolved within the project team (PM + relevant coordinator).  
- **High priority:** Escalated to Sponsor within 24 hours; updates at next weekly meeting.  
- **Critical:** Immediate notification to Sponsor and Advisory Board; emergency meeting convened.

**Review Cadence:** Issues are reviewed in weekly team stand‑ups. The log is publicly visible to the team (Notion) but sensitive details are restricted.
